Our Story

Founded in 2008 by lifelong South Florida resident and HVAC specialist, Maria Lopez, ComfortPro HVAC began as a one-truck operation with a simple mission: deliver honest, reliable cooling and heating services with a personal touch. From the very first installation in a sunlit West Miami bungalow to helping families stay comfortable during hurricane-force heat, our focus has never wavered.

Humble Beginnings

Maria started ComfortPro out of her garage, answering calls at all hours and moonlighting on weekends. She believed that clear communication—no jargon—paired with rock-solid workmanship would earn lifelong customers. Within two years, word-of-mouth referrals had doubled her team from one truck to a three-technician crew.

Growth & Innovation

In 2012, we opened our first full-service office and invested in ongoing training for our technicians. We introduced the region’s first 24/7 Emergency Response Team—guaranteeing an arrival within two hours on any breakdown call. That same year, we launched our annual “Cool for the School Year” program, offering free tune-ups to local teachers.

Community & Values

ComfortPro isn’t just a business—it’s a neighbor. We partner with Miami Rescue Mission each summer to provide A/C units to families in need, and our technicians volunteer at Habitat for Humanity builds every quarter. Our core values—Integrity, Excellence, Compassion—guide every service call and office decision.

Looking Ahead

Today, with a fleet of eight service vans and over 20 trained professionals, ComfortPro HVAC remains family-owned and operated. We’re excited to roll out our new Smart-Maintain™ Subscription Plan next year, combining IoT-enabled maintenance reminders with priority scheduling. No matter how fast technology moves, our promise endures: to keep South Florida comfortable, one home at a time.
